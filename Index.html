The Sriram Millenium School is a school located in Noida, UP, India. School was established in 2009.The Shriram Millennium School, Noida is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) and offers ICSE and ISC Curriculum. The Noida campus is also a Cambridge authorised school. It is affiliated to Cambridge Assessment International Education and offers the Cambridge Lower Secondary and IGCSE programmes. The Sriram Millenium School's present principal is Mrs. Uttara Singh
